Python

A snake is a legless reptile with a long, scaly body. It inhabits various environments such as forests and deserts and feeds on small animals. Snakes are known for their ability to shed their skin entirely in a process called molting.

  • Scientific Name: Python
  • Length: 13 meters
  • Lifespan: 20 to 30 years
  • Habitat: Forests and Deserts

If you see a snake, stay calm and move away slowly without sudden movements. Avoid touching or trying to kill it, and report it to the authorities if it's in a residential area. Wear protective footwear in nature to reduce risks.
